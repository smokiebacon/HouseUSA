Real Estate Services

HouseUSA Realty & Mortgage is a realty firm situates in the city of Arcadia, California. We specialize in residential, commercial, and land development projects. We are not only local real estate company; we are also affiliates and co-op with real estate companies in many states within US and Asian countries to service all our clients' needs in global wise.

There is a division of company in providing foreign financing services to foreign buyers as well as conventional loan programs and others. Our education division for pre-licensing courses and other training classes for all in- house agents which offer every week nights and weekends. The global business division of company is available with many investment projects and business opportunities for short and long term investment. We sell, buy, develop land of real estate in community and oversea. We also do staging for high end properties, marketing, advertisement, and publicity globalize for all in-house Listings. We welcome all agents who would like to experience the different working atmosphere to join with us! Our 24 hours hotline and Apps will always be available to answer all clients' concerns and questions. There are several Apps where you can reach us: Wechat, Line, and Messenger


Feel free to contact us for the initial appraisal of your home value today.
